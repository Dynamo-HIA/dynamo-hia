For the selected disease, enter relative risk of obtaining the selected disease given a unit increase in the continuous risk factor by age and sex (say, increase per BMI or litre of annual alcohol consumption). The risk factor has to be specified before creating this window. Other options are categorical and compounding risk factors.
Import-ready xml-files for this window can be created using the "Rel risk for riskfactor_Contin" worksheet in the Excel-file "Relative risk for riskfactor continuous.xls".
