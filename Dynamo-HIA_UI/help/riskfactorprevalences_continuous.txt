For the selected continuous risk factor, the distribution of the prevalence of the risk factor in the population by age can be specifying be entering the mean, standard deviation and skewness of either the normal or log-normal distribution.
The log-normal distribution is only defined for positive values which makes it more appropriate for some risk factors (e.g. BMI). 
Import-ready xml-files for this window can be created using the "Risk factor prevalence_Contin" worksheet in the Excel-file "Risk factor Continuous.xls".
