Enter the excess mortality for each sex and age class. This can be done either by choosing "Rate" or "Median Survival". 

Files of this format can be created using the "Disease mortality (per case)" worksheet in the "macro/2-Disease/Disease.xml" workbook.

Excess Mortality(rate) is the difference in mortality rate between those with the disease and those without the disease.

Excess Mortality(Median Survival) is the number of years from the onset of disease at which half of the not cured patients are still alive.

The Acutely Fatal is the percentage of the people that dies immediately of the disease.
The Cured Fraction is the percentage of patients that is cured, e.g. has a zero excess mortality.
