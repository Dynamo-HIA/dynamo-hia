For the selected continuous risk factor, enter the relative risk on total mortality by age and sex. 
For continuous risk factors, the increase per unit of risk factor is required. 
This captures the increased mortality of exposure to a risk factor in general and not just via the included diseases.
Import-ready xml-files for this window can be created using the "Rel risk for death_Contin" worksheet in the Excel-file "Relative risk for riskfactor continuous.xls".
