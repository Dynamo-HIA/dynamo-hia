For the selected categorical risk factor, enter the odds ratio on disability by age, sex, and risk factor class. 
This captures the increased risk of disability through the exposure to a risk factor in general and not just via the modelled diseases. These are needed to calculate summary measures.
Import-ready xml-files for this window can be created using the "Odds Ratio for disability_Cate" worksheet in the Excel-file "Relative risk for riskfactor cat.xls".
