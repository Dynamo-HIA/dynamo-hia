For the selected disease, enter relative risk of obtaining the selected disease given the risk factor class by age and sex. The risk of contracting a disease depends on the degree of exposure to a given risk factor. The risk factor has to be specified unter "Risk_Factors" before entering them for individual diseases. Other options are continuous and compounding risk factors.
Import-ready xml-files for this window can be created using the "Rel risk for riskfactor_Cat" worksheet in the Excel-file "Relative risk for riskfactor cat.xls".
